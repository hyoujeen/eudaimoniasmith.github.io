ABOUT


EUDAIMONIA at Smith College seeks to encourage human flourishing
among undergraduate students by opening them to the pursuit of
wisdom that is strongly encouraged by Smith's liberal arts curriculum.
Moreover, since today's various disciplines of study originated
from the study of "philosophy," Smith's Philosophy Department
seeks to bring together the different disciplines, in their own right,
into unique synergies within collegiate academia.

Not only do we hope to inspire intersectional conversations, but we
also hope to align with Smith's sustainability initiative "Green Smith"
by keeping this journal a solely virtual, "paper-less" source.
